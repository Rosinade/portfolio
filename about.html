Who am I?

I'm Rosanora - I love everything that have to do with product & visual design, to the design interface, all focused on the user experience.

I live by observation, empathy and intuition. Starting from there I aim to find a solution that “fits”, based on the particularity of each product and its context.

In the past 6 years as an art director, I have worked in model agency in Paris. I enjoy making complex matters simple and try as much as possible to contribute to projects that truly aim to have a positive impact in this world.

Thank you for stopping by!
